Byzantine Music Scales


Welcome! This is a small project I started to mainly help myself and other learners get to know and better understand the complex scales of Byzantine Music.
You can set your base (note) below, and then you can click the buttons for each note in each scale to hear what it would sound like. Hope this helps!
